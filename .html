THE PROCESS

Designing Thinking

Our process ensures that we create a website tailored to your business needs.

EMPATHIZE

Before we start, we determine if and how I can help you. What are your requirements for your new website? Why do you need a new website? What goals do you have, and what problems can we solve?

  • We get to know each other better
  • Determine how I can best assist you
  • Understand the goals you have for your website

DEFINE

Together, we develop a strategy that successfully combines your goals with the needs of your target audience. Based on this concept, I create the first wireframes and an interactive prototype.

  • UX Design
  • Wireframes
  • Interactive Prototype

IDEATE

Now comes the magic. Based on the previously developed concept, I create a high-end screen design perfectly tailored to your brand. A web design that sets you apart from your competition and provides an excellent user experience.

  • High-end web design tailored to your brand
  • Interactive prototype of the design

PROTOTYPE

In this step, we breathe life into your new high-end design. You will receive a custom-built website using a modular web design system and CMS integration. Animations will add the necessary flair to your site.

  • Custom-built website
  • Modular web design systems
  • CMS integration

TEST

In a personal Framer workshop, I will show you how to make changes to your new website quickly and easily. You will also receive personalized video tutorials.

  • Personal workshop
  • Personalized video tutorials
  • Edit text and images directly on your website
