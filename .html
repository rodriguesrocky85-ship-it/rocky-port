THE PROCESS

Design Thinking

My process focuses on creating websites that are thoughtfully designed and tailored to each project's goals and user needs.

01 — Understand

Every project begins with understanding the product, its users, and the challenges they face.

  • Product requirements analysis
  • User research and insights
  • Identifying pain points and opportunities

02 — Define

Based on research insights, I structure the user journey and define the core experience.

  • User flows
  • Information architecture
  • Wireframes

03 — Design

Transforming ideas into intuitive visual interfaces that align with the product’s goals and brand identity.

  • High-fidelity UI design
  • Design systems and components
  • Interactive prototypes

04 — Prototype

Bringing the experience to life through clickable prototypes and real interaction flows.

  • Interactive prototyping
  • Usability validation
  • Developer collaboration

05 — Iterate

Design is an ongoing process. Feedback and testing help refine the product experience.

  • Usability testing
  • Iteration and improvements
  • Continuous product enhancement
