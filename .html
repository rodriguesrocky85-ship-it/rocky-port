THE PROCESS

Designing Thinking

My process focuses on creating websites that are thoughtfully designed and tailored to each project's goals and user needs.

01 — EMPATHIZE

Before starting any project, I take time to fully understand the requirements and objectives behind it. I analyze the purpose of the website, the problems it should solve, and the audience it needs to serve.

  • Understand the project goals and requirements
  • Identify the target audience and user needs
  • Determine the best approach to solve the problem through design

02 — DEFINE

Based on the research and insights gathered, I develop a clear strategy that aligns project goals with user expectations. I begin structuring the website experience through early design planning.

  • UX design planning
  • Wireframe creation
  • Interactive prototype development

This stage helps establish a strong foundation for the website’s structure and user interface.

03 — IDEATE

Once the concept is defined, I design visually engaging and user-focused interfaces. My goal is to create designs that not only look modern and professional but also enhance usability and brand identity.

  • High-quality web design tailored to the project
  • UI layouts aligned with user experience principles
  • Interactive design prototypes

04 — PROTOTYPE

In this phase, I transform the design into a functional website. I develop the interface using modern tools and modular design systems to ensure flexibility and scalability.

  • Custom-built website development
  • Modular design system implementation
  • CMS integration where required

Animations and interactions are carefully implemented to enhance the overall experience.

05 — TEST

Before final delivery, I test the website to ensure usability, performance, and responsiveness across devices. I also ensure that the website is easy to manage and update.

  • Usability and performance testing
  • Responsive layout verification
  • Easy content editing and management

The final result is a clean, efficient, and user-friendly website that performs well and is simple to maintain.
