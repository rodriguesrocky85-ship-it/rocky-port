THE PROCESS

Edtech

Product design system and experience screens for a modern, scalable education platform.

Project Overview

A web and mobile learning platform designed for UPSC coaching institutes to provide students with access to lectures, practice exams, performance tracking, and academic schedules. The platform connects students and faculty through a centralized digital environment that simplifies course management and learning progress tracking.

ROLE
UI/UX Designer
RESPONSIBILITIES

TOOLS
Figma Photoshop
PLATFORM
Mobile Application + Web Platform

Problem

The coaching institute lacked a centralized digital platform for managing lectures, exams, and student progress. Students had to rely on multiple sources for study materials and schedules.

Key challenges

  • No digital system for accessing lectures and course content
  • Difficulty managing exam schedules and performance tracking
  • Limited communication between students and faculty
  • Fragmented learning experience across different platforms
  • Students needed a single platform to manage their preparation journey

Research

To design an effective learning platform, the workflows of both students and faculty were analyzed.

Key insights

  • Students needed quick access to lectures, exams, and results
  • Progress tracking and scoreboards were important for motivation
  • Faculty needed tools to upload lectures, manage exams, and monitor student performance
  • Navigation needed to be simple and structured for intensive exam preparation

User groups

  • Students – accessing lectures, exams, and performance tracking
  • Faculty – managing course content, exams, and student progress

Solution

I designed a unified EdTech platform that supports the complete learning workflow for both students and instructors.

Key features

  • Lecture access system for streaming course videos
  • Online exam module for practice tests and mock exams
  • Scoreboard and performance tracking for monitoring progress
  • Study schedule management for structured preparation
  • Separate login interfaces for students and faculty
  • Design prioritized clarity, accessibility, and ease of navigation

The platform provided a centralized digital environment for students preparing for competitive exams.

Key improvements
