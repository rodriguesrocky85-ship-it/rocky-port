UI / UX CASE STUDY

Asset Management App

Asset lifecycle tracking with request flows, approvals, and job operations designed for speed and clarity.

Project Overview

A mobile application designed for infrastructure teams to register and monitor physical assets used in highway operations. Field workers can track asset usage cycles, create job requests, and update asset status directly from the site. The application helps bridge the communication gap between field operations and management by providing real-time asset updates.

ROLE
UI/UX Designer
RESPONSIBILITIES
UX Research, User Flows, Wireframing, UI Design, Prototyping
TOOLS
Figma Photoshop
PLATFORM
Mobile Application

Problem

Asset management was handled manually using spreadsheets and paper records. This created several operational challenges for field teams and management.

Key challenges

  • Manual counting of assets on-site
  • Inaccurate asset records and miscounts
  • Difficulty tracking asset lifecycle and usage cycles
  • Lack of real-time updates for management
  • Operational delays due to mismanagement

Research

Understanding the workflow of field employees was critical to designing the solution.

Key insights

  • Field teams needed a quick and simple way to register assets on-site
  • Asset updates needed to be fast and accessible in mobile environments
  • Workers needed clear visibility of asset status and job assignments

User groups

  • Field operators managing asset registration
  • Operations teams monitoring asset status

Solution

I designed a mobile application that digitized the entire asset tracking workflow.

Key features

  • Asset registration system for quickly adding new assets
  • Asset status tracking to monitor lifecycle and usage cycles
  • Job creation and task assignment related to asset operations
  • Simple mobile navigation optimized for field environments
  • Interface optimized for speed, clarity, and minimal input effort

Outcome

The mobile solution digitized field operations and improved accuracy in asset tracking.

Key improvements

  • Faster asset registration on-site
  • Reduced manual errors in asset counts
  • Improved coordination between field teams and management
